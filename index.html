The story of Royaltea?

Starting from Guangzhou City in China, RoyalTea   is spreading around the world: with 600 stores across Asia and outlets in Canada and the USA. South Africans now have the chance to enjoy this new style of tea. 

Our signature drink: Cheese

How we create happiness

  1. SPECIALLY SELECTED TEAS  - We use only the best teas from China, Japan and India that contain no artificial flavours or preservatives for a unique tea experience.

  2. FRESH INGREDIENTS - Depending on your drink, we then combine it with fresh fruits, chinese brulee or Nutella. Dinks can be served hot or cold. 

  3. DELICIOUS TOPPINGS - The signature Royaltea topping is a soft cream cheese foam. On top of this you can also add Oreo, Tim Tam or Kit Kat crumbs!

    The options are endless...

Using only the freshest 

We use only the best ingredients sourced from local farmers in the Cape Town area. We use fresh tealeaves that are selected from all over the world that contain no artificial flavours or preservatives.
