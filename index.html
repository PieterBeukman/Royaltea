How we create happiness

  1. SPECIALLY SELECTED TEAS  - We use only the best teas from India, Thailand and China that contain no artificial flavours or preservatives for a unique tea experience.

  2. FRESH INGREDIENTS - Depending on your drink, we then combine it with fresh fruits, chinese brulee or Nutella. Dinks can be served hot or cold.
